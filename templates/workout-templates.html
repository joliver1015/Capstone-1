Workout Templates

When planning a strength training schedule, it's important to allow for 48-72 hours rest for the muscle group trained. Below are some examples of ways to optimally space out muscle groups trained based on how manuy days you plan on training.


2x Week Templates:
  • Day 1: Full Body
    Day 2: Full Body
  • Day 1: Upper Body
    Day 2: Lower Body

3x Week Templates
  • Day 1: Full Body
    Day 2: Full Body
    Day 3: Full Body

  • Day 1: Upper Body Push
    Day 2: Upper Body Pull
    Day 3: Lower Body

  • ABA Workout Template

    With this format you will have a Workout A and Workout B. The first week will be Workout A 2 times week and Workout B 1 time.The Following week you will do Workout B 2 times and A one time.

    Some examples of this Include:

    Workout A: Upper Push/Lower Pull Workout B: Upper Pull/Lower Push

    Workout A: Push Workout B: Pull

4 x Week Templates
  • Day 1 and Day 3: Upper Body
    Day 2 and Day 4: Lower Body

  • Day 1: Chest/Triceps
    Day 2: Back/Biceps
    Day 3: Legs/Abs
    Day 4: Shoulders
5 x Week Templates
  • Day 1: Chest
    Day 2: Back
    Day 3: Legs
    Day 4: Shoulders
    Day 5: Arms/Abs
