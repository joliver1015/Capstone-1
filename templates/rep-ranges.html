Rep Ranges and 1RM Calculator

Picking the right number:

It is important to make sure that your strength workouts reflect what you want to get out of it. Having the right amount of sets and repetitions at the appropriate amount of weight is key to efficiently reach your goals.

Strength:

Number of Sets: 2 to 6

Number of Reps: 1 to 6

Percent of 1 Rep Max: 85-100%

Rest Time Between Sets: 2 to 5 minutes


Hypertrophy:

Number of Sets: 3 to 6

Number of Reps: 6 to 12

Percent of 1 Rep Max: 70-85%

Rest Time Between Sets: 30 to 90 seconds


Endurance:

Number of Sets: 3 to 6

Number of Reps: 12 to 20

Percent of 1 Rep Max: 20-70%

Rest Time Between Sets: 30 to 60 seconds


Power:

Number of Sets: 3 to 5

Number of Reps: 1 to 5 for strength, 8 to 10 for speed

Percent of 1 Rep Max: 85% to 100% for strength, up to 10% body weight for speed

Rest Time Between Sets: 1 to 2 minutes, 3 to 5 for higher load/intensity
